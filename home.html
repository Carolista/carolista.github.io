A Lifelong Love Affair with Computers

My journey into tech has been almost entirely non-traditional, and a long time coming. From my earliest days learning DOS on a Tandy, to a couple of required CS classes in college, then 20 years in the nonprofit sector working with whatever cobbled-together technology I had available to me — I have always enjoyed computers and been more than comfortable learning new things on my own.

It took a battle with cancer in 2017 to wake me up to the realization that I wanted to do whatever it took to formally switch to a career in tech. Enter LaunchCode — and the many wonderful people who taught me, mentored me, and encouraged me along the way — and I had a strong start in my new career. As both software engineer and instructor, I love that I am constantly learning new things, and I am looking forward to continuing this journey ahead of me.
